Type Racer Clone

Overview

TypeRacer is a game where players type out a paragraph of text as quickly as possible. If the player makes any mistakes while typing, they must backspace and retype until they have typed the entire text correctly. Although the player may have corrected their mistakes, these mistakes still affect their accuracy. While typing, the player's words per minute (WPM) and accuracy is updated every second. Once the player has typed the full text, graphs of their accuracy and WPM over time are displayed.
